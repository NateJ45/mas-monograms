## 4 — The Running Stitch

"MAS" huge in Fraunces 700 ink with a wavy claret dashed stitch weaving through the baseline,
rising to a needle-eye loop; "MONOGRAMS" in tracked Mulish. Mark = giant M crossed by one bold
dashed stitch (the best 16px reduction of the five — worth remembering).

## 5 — The Cotton Boll Crest

Engraved cotton-boll emblem (paper lobes outlined in ink, brass calyx/stem — SC cotton as thread's
origin) over a letterspaced crest stack with brass rule and place-line. Charming story; the emblem
read as circles rather than cotton at header size.
